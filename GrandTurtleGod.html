Life as a High School Cross Country Runner

Why I Love Cross Country

Cross country is more than just running. It's about pushing yourself beyond limits you never thought possible, bonding with teammates, and feeling the satisfaction of crossing the finish line. Every race is a battle with yourself, but each practice makes you stronger, both mentally and physically.
